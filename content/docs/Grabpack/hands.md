Hands. How the grabpack interacts with things.

Before we continue, To equip any of the hands. Put either the Grabpack or the Hand you want to equip into your offhand then `right click` to equip it. To unequip, Make sure the Grabpack is in either your main hand or your offhand then `Shift + Rightclick`. The hand will be deposited into either your offhand or somewhere available in your inventory.

# Functions (WIP)
 Each hand has a function. lets see what each one does..

 ## Blue
 Used with its corrisponding hand scanner and for grabbing things.

 ## Red
 Used with its corrisponding hand scanner and for grabbing things.

 ## Green
 When you shoot this hand at a Green hand charger, It will gain a charge of FE for about 15 seconds. The timer is synced to the hand and the charger, The longer left on the timer the longer the Distributor will store the FE and output redstone. Can be used for grabbing and swinging.

 ## Flare
 Shoots flares. Deals damage. Takes about 30 ticks to recharge. Holds 5 Flares. If held in offhand without equiping it to your Grabpack, You can shoot yourself.

 ## Purple
 When shot at the purple hand-pads, You will be launched into the air. Distance traveled upwards is determined by if your already in the air and your current momentum.
